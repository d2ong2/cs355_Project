Galbi

beef short-lip, garlic, orion, apple, pear, soy sauce


Marinated Short-lip

Koreans have long liked ribs to the extent that the word galbi appeared in the Seungjeongwon Diary on June 24, 1639, the 17th year of King Injo's reign (1639). Since the mid-1920s, grilled beef ribs have become a full-fledged food-out culture.
